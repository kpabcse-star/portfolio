THE SATHWIK CINEMATIC UNIVERSE (SCU)

1. Alpha

Script Completed
Genre: Psychological Thriller | Location: Vijayawada & Machilipatnam

Logline: Andrea Phelosis is a fearless criminal lawyer practicing in Vijayawada who frequently consumes alcohol to cope with her high-stakes environment. Her world collides with Prasad, a man deeply terrified by her intense presence yet gradually growing to love her, after she stands up for him against a group of local goons humiliating him in his own office.

2. Man vs Guilty

Script Completed
Genre: Drama / Psychological | Location: Coastal Andhra

Logline: Ajay is a reckless bike racer whose life takes a sharp turn toward discipline and responsibility through his connection to his bike. Unbeknownst to him, his personal assistant Swapna is actually his cousin, harboring deep childhood resentment over a broken doll incident. Seeking vengeance, Swapna sabotages his progress by hiring men to burn his prized bike to ashes, getting him terminated from his professional deadline—only to find herself facing severe psychological guilt splashing water on her face in the washroom as she recalls how much he cared for her since childhood.

3. Greedy Love vs Lonely Man

In Development
Genre: Action Thriller / Sequel-Prequel | Location: Machilipatnam

Logline: Serving as both a prequel and direct sequel to Man vs Guilty, this story explores Ajay and Swapna's deep childhood bonds before tracing her father's toxic manipulation to marry her off to his personal choice, Rahul. While Ajay treats Rahul as a true friend, Rahul secretly treats him as a bitter enemy, using the old childhood doll incident to drive a wedge between the couple. Under immense family pressure, Swapna unexpectedly terminates Ajay from his company despite his massive support from 200 loyal employees, forcing a massive final confrontation.

💥 The Grand SCU Crossover Climax

The climax peaks dynamically in the coastal town of Machilipatnam where Ajay and Rahul face off while Swapna watches silently from the audience, tears filling her eyes. Just as Ajay faces near-defeat, Lawyer Andrea Phelosis arrives on the scene. She tears down Rahul's false friend persona, exposing his deep criminal roots from scratch as an international militant in Iran. Witnessing a beaten Ajay, a broken Swapna screams emotionally for him to rise. Re-energised, Ajay stands up and definitively takes down Rahul using his licensed personal firearm.
